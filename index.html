The Importance of Cybersecurity

Cyber threats are rapidly evolving, from phishing and ransomware to malware and social engineering. Hackers exploit weaknesses in systems, software, and human behavior to gain unauthorized access to sensitive information. One of the most common tactics is phishing, where attackers trick individuals into sharing confidential data through fake emails or links. Similarly, ransomware attacks lock critical files, demanding a ransom for recovery.
